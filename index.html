Project: P-Dex

P-dex is a full stack application, built using Mongoose, Express, Handlebars, and Node. P-Dex is designed for adding, editing, and removing pokémon information.To get started: Enter the Dex by clicking on the pokéball. Once you have entered the Dex, click on the 'Add A Pokémon' button to add pokémon. Fill out the information on the add page and submit. Once your pokémon has been added to the list, they may be edited by clicking on the specific pokémon. Image links can be found on Bulbapedia. Just right click and select 'Copy Image Address'.
